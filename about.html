OUR STORY

Passion For Food

Shakir Hussain is passionate about his food, though his field is engineering, which led him to try new dishes and recipes with his friends and family. At first, he started making different creative dishes with his friends during his hunting trips. His friends liked and praised his dishes which gave him the inspiration to come and serve the local community with his authentic flavors of Hyderabad. He is known for using 100% Zabiha Halal meat and fine quality ingredients to make his dishes on the menu.

His passion towards authenticity and flavor from his hometown Hyderabad made him start his catering business. He is licensed, insured to perform any wedding events and live bbq all across the United States!

Thanks to our loyal customers, Hussain Catering has become a favorite for many food eaters across the Nation. At Hussain Catering, we strive and dedicate ourselves to maintain our quality and keep our customers satisfied.
